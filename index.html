Q: Are the therapist photos real?
Yes. Photos are real shots with basic color adjustment only (no heavy filters). If you have preferences, tell us and we’ll try to match you with a suitable therapist.
Q: How early should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1 hour in advance, earlier for evenings or weekends. We don’t encourage walk-ins to protect privacy and control the number of clients.
Q: Any hidden charges?
No. Prices vary by treatment and duration. We will send you the rate before you confirm. No forced packages, no surprise add-ons. Any change/extension is always confirmed first.
Q: I have a medical condition. Can I still get a massage?
If you have heart disease, high blood pressure, recent surgery, or other serious conditions, please tell us in advance. Safety first — we may suggest postponing and following your doctor’s advice.
